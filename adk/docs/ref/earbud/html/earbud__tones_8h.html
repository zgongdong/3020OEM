Play the tone passed to this function.

Parameters
toneThe tone to play.
interruptibleIf TRUE, always play to completion, if FALSE, the tone may be interrupted before completion.
queueableIf TRUE, tone can be queued behind already playing tone, if FALSE, the tone will only play if no other tone playing or queued.
client_lockIf not NULL, bits set in client_lock_mask will be cleared in client_lock when the tone finishes - either on completion, when interrupted, or if the tone is not played at all, because the UI is not currently playing tones.
client_lock_maskA mask of bits to clear in the client_lock.
